TNPL Spectrum

TNPL Spectrum is a versatile, high-quality copier paper designed for modern office needs. It combines superior runnability with eco-friendly production, making it the smart choice for high-volume printing.

Multi-Purpose: Excellent for laser, inkjet, fax, and copiers.

Jam-Free: Precision cut for smooth, trouble-free performance.

High Brightness: 88% ISO brightness for crisp contrast.

Eco-Friendly: 100% Elemental Chlorine Free (ECF) and Acid-Free.

Technical Specifications

Basis Weight 70 / 75 / 80 GSM
Size A4 (210 x 297mm)
Brightness 88% ISO
Finish Smooth
Ream Packing 500 Sheets
Material Eco-friendly Paper / Wood-free
Sustainability

Greener Paper Solutions

TNPL leads the way in eco-friendly paper production, utilizing bagasse and sustainable energy to protect our planet.

Agro-Based Material

Manufactured primarily from bagasse (sugarcane residue), reducing dependency on forest resources.

ECF Bleaching

Uses Elemental Chlorine Free bleaching processes to minimize environmental impact.

Sustainable Energy

Powered by self-generated energy from wind farms and bio-methanation projects.
